I offer flexible pricing options including hourly rates, fixed project fees, and monthly retainer packages. The pricing depends on the scope of work, complexity, and time required. I'll provide a detailed quote after understanding your specific needs.

I'm flexible with communication methods and can use your preferred platform such as email, Slack, Zoom, Skype, or any project management tool. I provide regular updates and am always available for quick questions or discussions during my working hours.
