Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? I've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact me.

What services do you offer as a Virtual Assistant?

As a Virtual Assistant, I offer a wide range of services including email management, calendar organization, data entry, customer support, social media management, content creation, research, and more. I can tailor my services to meet your specific needs.

What types of websites can you develop?

I specialize in creating responsive websites for small businesses, portfolios, blogs, and e-commerce stores. I work with WordPress, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to deliver websites that are not only visually appealing but also functional and user-friendly.

How do you charge for your services?

I offer flexible pricing options including hourly rates, monthly retainer packages, and project-based pricing. The specific rate depends on the scope of work, complexity, and time commitment required. I'm happy to discuss your needs and provide a customized quote.

How quickly can you complete a website project?

The timeline for website projects varies depending on the complexity and scope. A simple website might take 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects could take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer ongoing support after completing a project?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages for websites I've developed. This includes regular updates, security checks, content updates, and technical support. For virtual assistance services, I'm available for both short-term and long-term engagements.
